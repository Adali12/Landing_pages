About the Rwanda ECD

The ECD Dashboard is an information hub to show progress in achieving results for children in early childhood health and development. It provides national, district, facility and community stakeholders with data and analysis from across sectors to inform their decision-making. The Dashboard is spearheaded by the National ECD Programme (NECDP) in partnerships with other sectors that provide essential services for young children, including early learning, maternal and child health,social protection, agriculture, and water, sanitation and hygiene.


The ECD dashboard includes progress accountability reports to allow a snapshot of progress across sectors in achieving results for young children. Access progress accountability reports here.
